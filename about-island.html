About Taniti

Key information to know before you visit.

Weather

Tropical climate with warm temperatures year-round and brief rain showers.

Safety

Generally safe for visitors; standard travel precautions still recommended.

Power & Currency

Standard power outlets; local currency used with most major cards accepted.

Health & Hospitals

Local medical facilities available for routine care and emergencies.

Local Rules

Respect local customs; observe posted guidance at beaches and public spaces.

Getting Around

Public transit, taxis, and walking paths connect major areas.
